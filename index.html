About Us

Bunn Gayo is a trusted coffee supplier specializing in the rich, diverse flavors of Indonesia's most renowned coffee regions: Gayo, Mandheling, and Lintong. Established with a passion for quality and sustainability, we are dedicated to bringing the finest Arabica beans from the highlands of Sumatra to coffee enthusiasts worldwide. At the heart of our operations is a commitment to preserving the heritage and uniqueness of Indonesian coffee.
